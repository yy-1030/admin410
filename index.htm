My Lovely Pet
Company History
My Lovely Pet is a newly established UK pet supplier. Our company was set up by Agnes Holmes in 2017. Our founder creates the company with the aim to provide a wide range of pet supplies for our lovely animal family members. Developing from selling dog supplies at the beginning year, the company now expand its specialization to cat and fish supplies including cat toys, cat food, fish food and fish tanks.
Why Use Us

We provide a wide range of pet supplies to give you as many choices as you want. With a high quality and favourable price, our company won the first prize for “Popular pet store chosen by Londoners of 2019”. Located in central London next to the Liverpool Street Station, our store attracts large volume of daily customer flow with convenient access by all kinds of transport. We also expand our service to Manchester and Edinburg offline branches. Our next goal is to establish online platform to give customers more convenient purchasing experience and have products delivered directly home.
